About CycleOlogy

Welcome to CycleOlogy bike shop located in Celbridge, Co Kildare Ireland.

CycleOlogy are open 6 days a week from 9.30 am to 6.00pm Monday to Friday .We are open on Saturday from 9.30am to 5 pm and closed on Sunday. We are just 20 minutes drive from Dublin.
